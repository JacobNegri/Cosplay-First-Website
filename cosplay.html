CosPlay?

Cosplay, (meaning costume play), is a performance art in which participants called cosplayers wear costumes and fashion accessories to represent a specific character. Cosplayers often interact to create a subculture. A broader use of the term "cosplay" applies to any costumed role-playing in venues apart from the stage. Any entity that lends itself to dramatic interpretation may be taken up as a subject and it is not unusual to see gender roles switvhed within the world of cosplay.

Favorite Sources for Cosplay:

  • Manga
  • Anime
  • Comic Books
  • Cartoons
  • Video Games
  • Live-Action Films
  • Television Series

There has been a rapid growth in the number of people cosplaying as a hobby since 1990. Cosplay has become a significant aspect of popular culture in Japan, as well as in other parts of Asia. The phenomenon has also reached the western world.

Cosplay event are common features of fan conventions and there are also dedicated conventions and local and international competitions, as well as social networks, websites and other forms of media centered on cosplay activities.
